## Common Questions Answered

**Q: Will AI take my job?**
A: AI changes how we work, rarely eliminates jobs entirely. Those who learn AI will have an advantage.

**Q: Is it cheating to use AI?**
A: Using AI as a tool isn't cheating (like using a calculator). Claiming AI work as entirely yours without disclosure can be.

**Q: Do I need to pay for AI?**
A: No! Free versions are excellent for beginners. Upgrade only if you need advanced features.

**Q: How much time will I save?**
A: Most beginners save 3-5 hours weekly. Power users save 10-15+ hours.

**Q: What if I make mistakes?**
A: Mistakes are how you learn! Experiment freely—you can't break anything.
